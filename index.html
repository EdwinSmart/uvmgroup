Our Services

Making a Difference

Food & Shelter

UVM Charity Group provides food and shelter to those in need, ensuring no one goes hungry or without a safe place to live.

Medicine & Medical

At UVM Charity Group, we provide access to medical care and essential medicines, ensuring the health of our communities.

Education

UVM Charity Group is dedicated to providing quality education and educational resources to empower Kenyan youth.

Clean Water

UVM Charity Group ensures access to clean water, promoting good hygiene and health in our communities.
